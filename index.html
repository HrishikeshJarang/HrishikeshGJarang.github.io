Heavy-Duty Glass Transport Rack (400 kg Capacity)

  • Load capacity: 400 kg
  • 2 multi-direction caster wheels for smooth movement
  • 2 stopper wheels to prevent unintended motion
  • Reinforced steel frame for load distribution
  • Optimized for shop-floor and industrial environments
