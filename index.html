5. Heavy-Duty Glass Handling Rack (400 kg Capacity)

Description: Designed and manufactured a heavy‑duty glass rack capable of safely handling up to 400 kg glass sheets. The rack includes a reinforced tubular frame structure, engineered load-bearing supports, and anti-slip base to ensure safe storage and movement.

Features:

  • Designed using structural steel sections for high strength.
  • Equipped with 2 multi‑direction wheels for easy maneuvering.
  • Includes 2 stopper wheels to lock the rack and prevent movement during loading/unloading.
  • Optimized support base ensures uniform load distribution for glass sheets.
  • Transparent 3D visualization of glass placement for safety inspection.

Tools: SolidWorks

Key Skills: Structural design, load calculation, weldment design, mechanical safety standards
